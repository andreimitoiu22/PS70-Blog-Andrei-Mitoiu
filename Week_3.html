### Photos <div class="two-image-row"> <img src="./Images_week_3/20220916_170707.jpg" class="half-width-image"/> <img src="./Images_week_3/20220916_182105.jpg" class="half-width-image"/> </div> This was the initial plan. As seen in [Sophie's flock of birds](https://sophiecwebster.github.io/PHYSCI-70/03_kinetic/index.html), I wanted to create a system that transformed rotational motion into linear motion using wires that are bent in the right way. My plan was to immitate the movement in a "wack a mole" game, in which moles randomly come out of the ground. Unfortunately, I knew I couldn't immitate the randomness, so I wanted to just have them come up and down one after the other.
